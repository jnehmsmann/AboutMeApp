The Life.

an epic tale

The start of our epic tale begins on the eve of All Hollow's Eve when a plucky, bright-eyed young lass was born into this world. It was destined from that moment that that baby girl would one day become the best web designer that the world had ever seen.

She grew up in a town house in Maryland with her mom, dad, and little sister (and the occassional pet). She went to private school for elementary until 6th grade when her family moved to Pennsylvania. She graduated high school in 2010 with a set goal in mind to become a web designer.

The road was not an easy one. Though she knew what she wanted to do, she had no idea how or where to obtain it. She started off taking a couple classes at the local community college, afraid that she would have to settle for a major in something she didn't exactly want.

That's when her friend told her how he had some friends at his school that were in the major she wanted. She looked into it, and a year and a half later, wound up at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. Since then, she's been improving and expanding her knowledge and abilities.

And although her web designing skills are great, she still has a lot to learn before she can help anyone. But I believe Jordyn can save the world.1

1 Quote from intro sequence in "Avatar: The Last Airbender".
